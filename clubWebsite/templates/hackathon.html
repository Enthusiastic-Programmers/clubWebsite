About

Moorpark is hosting one of the first Ventura County based hackathon for college students. For two days, students will compete and team together to create innovative and amazing products that will benefit everyone as a whole. Through the creativity of creation,communication of ideas, and lots of coffee,Hack805 wants to set the level of what a community college hackathon can be like.
